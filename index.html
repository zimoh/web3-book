Chapter Overview

Eight chapters. One coherent arc.

From foundational concepts to frontier applications — structured for a full semester or intensive short course.

01

The Web3 + AI Opportunity

Why AI and blockchain are converging, and what it means for entrepreneurs. The macro forces shaping the decentralized AI internet.

02

Blockchain Fundamentals for Entrepreneurs

The technical foundations every venture builder needs — cryptography, consensus, smart contracts — without requiring a CS degree.

03

Tokenomics — Designing Your Token Economy

How to design, model, and evaluate token systems using original frameworks including the Token Business Model Canvas (TBMC).

04

Business Models for Web3 Ventures

From DeFi protocols to creator economies — a taxonomy of Web3 revenue models with real-world cases and analysis frameworks.

05

Governance and DAOs

Designing decision-making systems for decentralized organizations. Voting mechanisms, treasury management, and governance failures.

06

Fundraising in Web3

ICOs, IDOs, grants, and hybrid models — navigating the Web3 capital landscape and structuring your fundraising strategy.

07

Building and Growing Your Web3 Community

Community as competitive advantage — strategies for acquisition, engagement, retention, and measuring community health.

08

The Future of Web3 Entrepreneurship

Autonomous agents, machine economies, and what comes after Web3 — building ventures for the decade ahead.

About the Author
Zishan A. Mohammad
ReTech Fellow · École des Ponts Business School
Researcher · Entrepreneur · Educator

Zishan A. Mohammad is a researcher and educator at the intersection of entrepreneurship, blockchain, and artificial intelligence. His work spans empirical token economy research, Web3 venture strategy, and the design of decentralized AI governance systems.

With academic roots at École des Ponts ParisTech - Business School and University of Durham and a career spanning research, teaching, and venture consulting, Zishan brings both analytical rigour and practitioner experience to this textbook — bridging the gap between cutting-edge academic research and the realities of building Web3 ventures.
