The definitive guide to building ventures where AI meets the decentralized web.

Two seismic forces are reshaping entrepreneurship: artificial intelligence and Web3. Separately, each creates enormous opportunity.   • Design token economies using the Token Business Model Canvas (TBMC)
  • Navigate DAO governance, DeFi fundraising, and regulatory strategy
  • Build and scale Web3 communities from zero to network effect
  • Explore frontier opportunities: DePIN, AI agents, and the space economy
