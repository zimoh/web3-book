4.3 Revenue Models in Web3
Unlike traditional SaaS or marketplace models, Web3 ventures often generate revenue through protocol fees, token appreciation, and transaction-based mechanisms. The choice of revenue model is deeply intertwined with token design and governance structure, creating a unique interplay between economics and technology.
Key Insight
Protocol revenue differs from traditional revenue in a fundamental way: it flows transparently through smart contracts, making it auditable by any stakeholder in real time.
